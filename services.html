Process

Record Audio

Record your podcast or other audio on a digital voice recorder or smartphone. We can accept all audio and video formats.

Message Us For Quote

Use the contact form for a personalised quote. Include a link to a sample of the audio to be transcribed, we do not work with poor audio.

Upload Audio

We use a secure cloud network to store audio files. We can also work with a link to any audio, we will download the audio at no extra cost.

We Transcribe

Sit back and relax whilst we do our thing. Our transcriptions are carried out by humans who drink a lot of coffee.

We Send Invoice

Once the transcription is complete, we generate an invoice via TransferWise or PayPal. Your payments are protected.

Receive Transcription

You will receive your transcript in MS Word.doc unless requested otherwise.
