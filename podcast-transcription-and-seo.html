Podcast Transcription and SEO

08ndMay 2018

By Haydn Hadley

If you producing a podcast, then you likely have become familiar with the term ‘SEO’. SEO, which stands for search engine optimization, is a system of producing content that is likely to be found when a person searches certain keywords on Google, Yahoo or Bing. This article is not going to teach you how to rank first on search engines overnight. But, while it is a continually changing science, there are a few things about SEO that are known truths:

Quality Content is King (or Queen)

Regularly updating relevant, quality content on your website is the best way to ensure that you work your way up the search engine standings. So the regularly running podcast is a great thing, most professionals say that 2-6 time's a month is optimal.

Choosing the Right Keywords and Search Phrases is Important

Using the right keywords in your podcast, and in the title can help to ensure that your intended audience is able to find your new content. It’s important to not cram too many keywords, or irrelevant keywords though. Search engines will penalise pages, whole websites even, that are deemed spammy.

The Search Engine Spiders Don’t Have Ears

Unfortunately, even your greatest podcast is going to fail if your only audience is the search engine spiders. The pieces of code used to scour the internet for new content, called spiders, cannot decipher audio. Those spiders are responsible for determining which content will be best for the search engine user, you want to be sure that they enjoy your content.

So What is a Podcaster to do?

In order to give your site an organic SEO boost, consider online transcription service. Digital transcription services can be used to create quick, accurate text versions of your podcasts. Our reliable professionals will type out each new podcast, with quick turnaround times, so that you can post an audio and a text version of the content. Your audience can still enjoy the usual format, but the spiders will be able to decipher what you have to say. They will pick up on those important keywords and that means that you have a better chance of ranking higher in the search results list.

Our podcast transcription packages are affordable, and well worth the investment, if it means that your audience grows.
